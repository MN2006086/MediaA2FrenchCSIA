French A2 Media
News Sources

Le Parisien

Le Parisien is a popular French newspaper focusing on local and national news. With a clear and direct writing style, it focuses on topics like politics, sports, culture and regional issue.

Characteristics: Clear and Direct, Slightly Complex, Highly Regional

Skills Improved: Grammar, Reading, Writing, Cultural Awareness



Le Gorafi

Le Gorafi is a satirical news site which mimicks actual news, but with an ironic twist. Gain an insight into French humour and satire and what issues French people are most focused on!

Characteristics: Simple language, short articles, variety of mediums.

Skills Improved: Reading, Cultural Awareness



France Bleu

France Bleu is an instance of regional and local French news, focusing on the issues a normal person would be concerned about, allowing for A2 learners the opportunity for great conversation starters!

Characteristics: Natural Language, Lots of Common French phrases and casual expressions

Skills Improved: Listening, Reading, Speaking, Cultural Awareness
